The Master Craftsman

Dennis
Umanzor.

"If your name is on the cabinet, quality cannot be negotiated."

Dennis Umanzor spent his formative years mastering European cabinet-making — an education in precision that became the backbone of everything Denni's Cabinets stands for. Since opening the Norcross studio in 2008, every project has been personally supervised by Dennis himself.

The difference lives in details invisible to most: dovetail joints on every drawer, solid wood interiors, a three-stage finish process that takes three times the industry standard. Every cabinet is a commitment — to the material, to the homeowner, and to the craft.
